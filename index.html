Collaborative grocery

It is pretty amazing when you think about it. It is as simple as putting your grocery list on the Internet for everyone to see. I mean it doesn't cost a dime so why not?

I'm sold. Let's do this. »

Why?

Because why not? It's not like you care about your privacy at all. This brings convenience front and center in your grocery shopping experience. You'll be able to use the power of GitHub for making changes to your grocery list. It is even better if you do groceries with your family.

Let's do this. »

Be as active as you like

You can go out and find deals or you can step back and let your collaborators lead you into savings. Show us your frugal side and lead the community or simply work on your cancer research and let the community leaders decide what's for dinner.
