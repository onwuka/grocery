What is Grocery?

Grocery (internal name) is a new, innovative way to do groceries with a boring name. Why should doing groceries be boring? If we are what we eat, shouldn't we have a bigger discussion on what we eat?

I'm sold. Let's do this. »

More about this program

Grocery is a rapidly-evolving platform for doing groceries. It is not a business at this point and it does not seek to make a profit. Grocery brings the power of GitHub. Users will be able to find best prices for the best produce and other groceries available in the market.

While it sounds to good to say that we are all unique as snowflakes, the truth is that as a species, we have roughly similar eating patterns. As such, it should be possible to find an optimal diet for a particular locale and demographic. Let's help one another out in this process of discovery.
